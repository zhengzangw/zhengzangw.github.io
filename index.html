About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at NUS, advised by Asst. Prof. Yang You. I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science from Nanjing University in June 2021. I spent a year in iCyPy at UC Berkeley from Apr. 2020, supervised by Prof. Alberto L. Sangiovanni-Vincentelli and Dr. Xiangyu Yue.

Research Interest

My research interest lies on Machine Learning (AI) and High-Performance Computing (HPC), with a particular focus on training models with large batch and training models that generalize well.
