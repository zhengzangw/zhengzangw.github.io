About Me

I am a undergraduate student of Department of Computer Science and Technology in Nanjing University, with an outstanding academic performance (92.2/100) in National Elite Program of Computer Science. From Apr. 2020, I am visiting iCyPy in UC Berkeley, supervised by Prof. Alberto L. Sangiovanni-Vincentelli and Dr. Xiangyu Yue.

Research Interest

My research interest lies on Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS), with a particular focus on Computer Vision, Domain Adaptation and Self-supervised Learning. My work has been focused on techniques for solving healthcare AI (computer vision for medical image processing), and on understanding the theoretical underpinnings of such techniques.
