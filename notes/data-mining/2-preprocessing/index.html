Data Transformation

  • Normalization

    • min-max

      $$ v'=\frac{v-\min_A}{\max_A-\min_A}(\text{new_max}_A-\text{new_min}_A)+\text{new_min}_A$$

    • zero-mean

      $$ v'=\frac{v-\overline{A}}{\sigma_A} $$

    • decimal scaling

      $$ v'=\frac{v}{10^j} $$
