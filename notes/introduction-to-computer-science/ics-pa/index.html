顺时针法则

  • 顺时针法则

  • [10]: an array 10 of …

  • (): Once you meet one of brace, then a pair of them are omitted

  • *: pointer to …

  • (params): a function which receives params and returning …

  • const(at the begining): const

  • const: constant …
